**Description** (max 4000 chars):
```
Group Point makes planning trips with friends actually simple.

No more 200-message group chats trying to figure out when everyone's free. Just mark the days you can't travel, and Group Point instantly shows the dates that work for the whole group.

HOW IT WORKS
• Create a group for your trip and invite friends with a simple code
• Everyone marks when they CAN'T go — it's faster than picking when you can
• Group Point highlights the best dates, when the most people are free
• Confirm the winning date together and start planning the fun part

FEATURES
• A clean, simple calendar — tap to mark Unavailable or Maybe
• Smart "best date" suggestions based on everyone's availability
• Set a trip length and date window to narrow things down
• Require a minimum headcount, so a date only counts when enough people can make it
• See every member's availability at a glance
• Great for weekend getaways, ski trips, bachelor parties, family reunions — any group plan

WHY GROUP POINT
Most scheduling tools are built for meetings. Group Point is built for trips — multi-day windows, headcounts, and the messy reality of getting friends to agree on a date.

Sign in securely with Apple, Google, or email. Your data is yours — delete your account anytime.

Find the date that works. Then go.
```
