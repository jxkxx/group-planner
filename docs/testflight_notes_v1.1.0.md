# Group Point 1.1.0 — TestFlight "What to Test"

Thanks for testing! This update reworks how availability works and adds group planning tools. Please try the following and tell me anything confusing or broken.

## 🗓️ Your personal calendar (big change)
It now works the opposite way: you mark the days you **can't** travel, not the ones you can.
- Tap a date once = **Unavailable** (red)
- Tap again = **Maybe Unavailable** (amber)
- Does this feel clear? Is the "Mark when you can't travel" wording obvious?

## 👥 Group calendar
- Create or join a group, then set your availability inside it.
- Try the new **"Select dates"** mode — tap it, then tap several days at once and apply a status in one go. Smooth or clunky?
- You should see a tip popup the first time. Did it make sense?

## ⚠️ Sync warning
- If you mark yourself *available* in a group on a day you set as *unavailable* personally, you should get a heads-up. Does that warning show up?

## ✅ Confirming a date
- When a group lands on a good date, members can **confirm** it. Try confirming with a couple of test accounts. When everyone confirms, you should get a prompt to block that time personally.

## 👤 Members & filtering
- Tap a member in the **Members** tab — you should see their dates broken down by status.
- Use the **dropdown** on the group calendar to choose which availability dots show.

## 🔢 Required headcount
- In a group's trip settings, set a minimum number of people. The calendar should shade dates greener as more people are free.

## What I most want to know
1. Does the "mark when you can't travel" idea click, or is it confusing?
2. Anything crash, freeze, or look broken?
3. Is creating + sharing a group easy?

Reply through TestFlight feedback or message me directly. 🙏
